How to Author a TechNet Wiki Article withwo types of TechNet Wiki URLs:

1. A URL with a number. Example: http://social.technet.microsoft.com/wiki/contents/articles/440.aspx

2. A URL with the name of the article. Example: http://social.technet.microsoft.com/wiki/contents/articles/how-to-author-a-technet-wiki-article-with-the-name-in-the-url.aspx



This article's URL includes the name of the title of the article.

To have the name of your article included in the URL (see #2 above), make sure you have no punctuation. When you add punctuation to your article, then your URL becomes a number instead.

When you remove the punctuation from your article, the URL returns to the name of your article.
