Introduction

  TechNet and MSDN forums are one and the same. We browse to the forums using the MSDN URL or using the TechNet URL, but the behind the scene these are one and the same, only the GUI interface is different. For the sake of this article, we will use the name TechNet instead to write both TechNet/MSDN. After all, the TechNet WIKI is more related to the TechNet community. 

There are probably several thousands of forums at the TechNet website, in different languages, categories, and fields of interests. Navigating between these in order to find the best fitting forum for our field of interest is almost impossible. There is no home page where we can search for a specific forum or see the entire list of forums. This article intends to change this and make our life a bit easier.

This article highlights a small portion of the TechNet list of Forums. In time, with the help of the TechNet members, we could improve this article and keep it updated. Who knows, maybe in the future this sentence will no longer be relevant as we will have added all the forums in our list. In the mean time, if you are looking for specific forum you can start by searching within this article.

  If you are familiar with any forum that is not in the list, please feel free to edit this article and add it. To make the article more readable, please keep the same format as the existing forums.
