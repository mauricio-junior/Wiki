Microsoft will no longer be supporting archiving of Bloomberg and legacy instant messaging data, although these types of data were originally supported by EHA.


For customer who are in need of archiving Bloomberg and legacy instant messaging data, we recommending considering the archiving solution provided by our partner, LiveOffice.


Microsoft and LiveOffice have pre-negotiated prices to help make transition efficient and prioritize transitioning customers.  Offers include:

LiveOffice FastPass Program

Microsoft and LiveOffice have established an efficient solution for transitioning archives between EHA and LiveOffice Discovery Archiving or AdvisorMail solutions.  This process will help minimize archive downtime while expediting the transition from one archive solution to another.  For more information on the process and how to request the LiveOffice FastPass please contact a LiveOffice representative.
