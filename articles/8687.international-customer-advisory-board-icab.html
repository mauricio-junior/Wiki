Have some opinions you'd like to share? We’re listening.

 

The Cloud + Enterprise Division here at Microsoft is building a worldwide community of IT pros who want to share their frank opinions about our products (primarily Windows Server, Microsoft System Center, Windows Azure, and SQL Server). We call it the International Customer Advisory Board (ICAB).

In the ICAB, we discuss Microsoft technology, troubleshoot technical issues, and weigh in on innovations in content and community—on two private, invitation-only online groups (Connect and Facebook). Members may also be invited to exclusive events at conferences like MMS, Tech Ed, and the MVP Summit.

What’s in it for you?

In addition to networking and troubleshooting, you’ve got Microsoft’s ear—and a whole new community of colleagues across the globe, which can be helpful when you decide to chuck it all and move to Paris or Hong Kong. What's in it for Microsoft, of course, is having a group of smart, dedicated people tell us what they really think. You'll be offering us a glimpse into what our customers want from our products, helping us make better products for you.

Interested?

If so, email icabmsft@microsoft.com with your name & business card details. We look forward to hearing from you.
