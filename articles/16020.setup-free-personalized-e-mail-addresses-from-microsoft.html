Microsoft providing e-mail accounts on your domain without any cost. And also providing all the Microsoft services that given to Microsoft normal accounts .Like Sky Drive ,Outlook etc. you can get this service from http://www.domains.live.com Click on get started to start register this service. In next page add domain that own you (please note this if you adding any subdomain like co.cu,cz.cc Microsoft may suspend your account later or will not allow to register) .If you don't have any domain you can purchase new domain by clicking link below. After adding your domain you have to verify your ownership of domain using MX record. To verify your domain ownership but there is an another TXT record verification method that valid only 30 days .If you choosing TXT verification method after 30 days you have to verify your MX record. In the verification page below you can see another TXT verification for your server trust it required to trust the e-mail is originated from your domain for other mail servers.

First to verify your ownership of domain go to your domain provider site example http://in.godaddy.com there you will find DNS configuration/settings or Zone record. Click on that and you will see options to create zone records. Their select record type as MX those who want to us TXT record select record select type as TXT .Go to domains. Live -> domain settings page .To create MX record copy the complete string after 'MX server:' (single line) paste in corresponding record field .To create TXT record copy the string after 'value:'(single line) and paste corresponding record field. Additionally to MX record set TTL:3600 or 1 Hour and priority 10. Next for server trust you have to create another TXT record same way ,copy the string after 'value:' under server trust (single line) paste on new TXT record field of your domain. And you have to also set TTL:3600 or 1 Hour and save all this record you created.

Back to domain settings of the domains.live.com and click on refresh button .If you added record correctly you will get verified and you can start registering e-mail addresses.
