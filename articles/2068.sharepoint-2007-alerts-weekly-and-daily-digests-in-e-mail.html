Problem

Weekly Alert Template does not show complete names of  the documents in the e-mail notifications.

 

Solution

Replace the digest tag in the AlertTemplate.xml as given below. This has been changed to check the field name and shows the title of document instead of item name which was original CAML. This change has helped me achieving the more appropriate email with complete names of document.
