Office 365: Grant Send As Permission Without Using PowerShell P1 Plan

Granting Send As permission without using PowerShell P1 Plan

  1. Login into your Microsoft Online Portal.
  2. Click Outlook.
  3. Click Options and select See All Options.
  4. Click Connected accounts.
  5. Click New.
  6. Enter the e-mail address of the other account you're connecting to, and the Password of this account then click next.
  7. Click My mail.
  8. Create a new message.
  9. Click From, and make your choice.
