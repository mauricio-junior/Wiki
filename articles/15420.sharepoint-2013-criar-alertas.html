Introduction:


SharePoint 2013 brings a very interesting feature, which we call Alerts . This feature is not new and was maintained from earlier versions such as SharePoint 2010. These alerts have as main objective to notify users via email or sms of any changes made at a particular site of the site, we can mention for example the libraries where documents of a company's project are stored or the lists that are usually used as registers of information. In this way, as soon as any item is modified in these locations, a notification will be issued to the people defined in the alert, this message will inform the date, time and the person who made the change. This conveys security and greater control on the part of those who administer the information and on the part of those who change it,

Example:



The When Send Alerts option allows you to basically set the frequency of sending alerts and notifications, this pulps resources and lets you make the most of the 2013 SharePoint alerts service. In this group you can find the following items:

Enviar notificação imediatamente: este recurso envia uma notificação assim que qualquer item for modificado. Pensando na estrutura da sua empresa e se você quiser trabalhar com essa opção lembre-se que ele vai requerer um processamento maior do seu servidor de email no entanto é a opção mais utilizada pelas empresas.

Enviar um resumo diário: esta opção armazena todos os registros de alterações feitas (data, hora e o nome da pessoa) e envia a você um resumo do dia com todas as modificações realizadas.

Send a weekly summary : this option stores all the change records made (date, time and person name) and sends you a summary on the date and time that was set in the When box , so you will have your weekly report changes that have been made.

Remember you can only use one option at a time.

When finished, click OK .
