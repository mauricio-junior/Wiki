How to open a GitHub Markdown link in a new tab

When you click links 1 or 2, you will be asked the first time what your preferences are for "new tab" links.

After your first choice, a cookie will remember for future links. You can click back in the history and try the same link again.

If you choose to stay on the same tab, clicking back in the history from the new page should skip the middle page and bring you back here.

If you choose 'new tab', the page will open in a new tab and the old tab will return here.

  • 1. Go to tab/passthrough to http://netities.com

  • 2. Go to tab/passthrough to netities.com

  • 3. Clear cookie to try again
